Read Me - Instructions & Credits



Controls:


WASD/Arrow Keys - Move
Left Click - Shoot LoveGun


Shift - Run

Space - Jump


Esc - Pause

How to Play:



You have been selected to undergo training for an offensive mission to Hateporium, the dreaded enemies of our nation, Lovetopia. Once in the Lovetopia Combat Simulator, fill all the enemies with Love by shooting them with the LoveGun to complete the training. Be careful you don't get filled with Hate from their HateGuns!
